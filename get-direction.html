What's your essence?

What is the heart of your product?

the problem

One of the biggest problems in delivering software (and products in general) is prioritization and goal setting. It's hard to prioritize when you're not clear on the core system and feedback loops.

If you find yourself struggling with regular post-production rework and overly complicated solutions, you most probably lack clarity of your core systems feedback loops and how each part relates to the bigger system.

the solution

A product teardown takes a critical look at your entire system from a systems thinking and usability perspective. It identifies your unique value proposition, your strengths, and what's missing.

Using the Game Thinking framework I identify your product's core systems diagram and main feedback loop. The result is a contextual map that helps you navigate complex systems and create coherent systems rather than feature factories.
